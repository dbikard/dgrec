encoding_RNA_pairing

 encoding_RNA_pairing (sequence_list:str)

Encodes a list DNA sequences based on base pair probability statistics. The DNA sequence here correspond to 20 bases of Avd + the TR sequence + 50/100/150 bases of the DGR spacer The encoding is independent of the TR length

Type Details
sequence_list str list of DNA sequences of TRs
